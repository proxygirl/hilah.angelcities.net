bio

hilah katherine t. (b. 1997) is a multi-media digital artist from the midwest where she has been writing, photographing and digitizing for most of her life. she attended the university of kansas, in her hometown, where she studied computer science, and then photography and creative writing.

hilah’s work is a personal archive of memories; moments that are blurred between the softness of the past and the dreary reflection of the present. she uses image-making and intimate essays as a way of carefully and quietlly understanding her life as she organizes it in her online world.
